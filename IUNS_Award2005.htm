2005 IUNS AWARD LECTURE

Tookplace on September 21, 2005 inDurban, South Africa at theInternational Convention Centre.  The lecture was titled "Good Governance forNutrition in the Philippines: Elements, Experiences and Lessons Learned."

                    

The 2005 IUNS Award sponsored by the  International NutritionFoundation will be Presented to Dr. Florentino Solon, Founder and Director ofthe Nutrition Center of the Philippines (NCP) and one of the world’s outstanding public health nutritionists. His development of foods for preschool,school emergency feeding and of innovated nutrition education materials havebeen major contributions to nutrition security in the Philippines.  He hasintroduced effective programs and policies for the welfare of mothers andchildren in the Philippines that have been widely promoted by internationalorganizations and benefited the programs of other nations. The applied nutritiontraining  programs of the NCP in collaboration with the United NationsUniversity and the University of Queensland have spread his influenceworld-wide.

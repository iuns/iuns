Guidelines for Financial Support to Adhering Bodies

At the IUNS Council Meeting in Osaka, Japan (March 2002), guidelines for financialsupport (Annual Dues) to Adhering Bodies were prepared and approved.

  ABs must submit a formalapplication for Council consideration

Support will be considered solelyfrom ABs in Category 1.

  ABs must provide evidence ofexistence (i.e. membership number, constitution, activity) and their actionplans.

ABs should provide the maximumpossible part of funding.

ABs must provide evidence ofability to sustain interest in IUNS (i.e., attendance at general assembly,commitment to payment of annual dues in the future).

Support will be reviewed on ayearly basis subject to availability of funds.

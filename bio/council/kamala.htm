EBiographical information for the NAAS Year Book

 

Krishnaswamy Kamala

FELLOW, ELECTED, 2003

 

Born in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, India on 4th April 1940.  Educated at Stanley Girls High School, Hyderabad; 1945-56; Women’s college, Hyderabad : 1956-1958, Osmania Medical College: 1956-1964, Post graduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Osmania Medical College, University of Osmania : 1966-1968.

 

Advisor / Consultant to Pharmaceutical / Food companies

 

Emeritus Medical Scientist of Indian Council of Medical Research : 2002-2005

Director of National Institute of Nutrition, Food and Drug Toxicology Research center, National center for Laboratory and Animal Science: 1997-2002

Senior Deputy Director in charge of Drug Division / Food and Drug Toxicology Research Center : 1992-1997

Assistant Research Officer to Assistant Director, National Institute of Nutrition : 1964-1992

 

Awarded : Rajammal Devdas Oration, of Avinashalingam Institute for Home Sciences and higher Education for Women, 2005; Certificate of Excellence, Indian Medical Association, World Woman’s Day, 2005; ASTC-Eminent Women Scientist Award 2004; S.G. Srikantia Memorial Lecture, of Nutrition society of India, 2004; Basant Devi Amir Chand Prize, of ICMR,1997; Distinguished Scientist for outstanding contributions in the field of Science & Technology, by AP Akademi of Sciences,1997; Chopra Oration, Indian Pharmacological Society, 1997; Basant Devi Amir Chand Prize, ICMR, 1997; CIBA-GEIGY Award for Nutrient Drug Interactions, 1989;  Dr.Kamala Menon Medical Research Award in the field of Internal Medicine, ICMR,1988; Dr. V.N. Patwardhan Prize in Nutritional Sciences, ICMR, 1977; Shakuntala Amir Chand Prize for Medical Research, ICMR, 1973; Young Scientist Award for Medical Research, ICMR, 1972;

 

Fellow : Third World Academy of Sciences, 2003

Executive Committee Member : International Union Of Nutritional Sciences

 

International Union of Nutritional Sciences, 2001; Indian National Science Academy, New Delhi, 1997; National Academy of Sciences, Allahabad, 1996; National Academy of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, 1994; AP Akademi of Sciences, Hyderabad, 1994; Indian Academy of Sciences, Bangalore, 1993;

 

Research Areas : Nutrition and Medical Sciences, Preclinical and Clinical Toxicology
